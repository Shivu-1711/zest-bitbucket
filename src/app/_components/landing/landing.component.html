About Us

ZEST is a third-party aggregator application of ZEST Solutions Inc. which endeavours to provide consumers with fitness at the convenience and needs of everyone while providing local gyms and activity studios with customers they want. The application brings together a number of GYMs, activity studios like Dance Studios, Martial Art Studios, Yoga Clubs, etc. under one platform so our members can choose where to go when to go, and only pay when they use without any hassle and issues. A membership is available both as a one-time pay per use Wallet Credits.

ZEST is a Sales Solution for GYMs and Fitness Studios that are looking to grow the customer base but lower customer acquisition costs. ZEST is providing the GYMs and other Studios with a subscriber base that is fitness conscious and is ever ready to use a fitness place if it is around when needed. GYMs and activity Studios can become a member by applying for Partnership which will be approved after consultation and agreement to terms and conditions between you and us.
